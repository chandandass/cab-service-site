Project: Abhi Cab Service website
Date: 2026-04-17

1. Goal
- Build a modern, visual, conversion-focused, SEO-friendly cab service website.
- Primary purpose: get direct inquiries and bookings through WhatsApp and phone calls.
- Website should rank for local taxi/cab search intent around Panchkula, Chandigarh, Mohali, Delhi route bookings, airport taxi, and "near me" queries.

  Panchkula
  Chandigarh
  Mohali
  Delhi route bookings
  Chandigarh airport taxi service
  Local and outstation cab service

4. Required Website Features
- Fully responsive layout for mobile and desktop.
- Premium, modern visual style.
- Strong SEO structure.
- Call button.
- WhatsApp deep link button.
- Pricing section visible and easy to find.

- User-grabbing sections and CTAs throughout the page.
- Config-driven content so phone number, WhatsApp number, prices, SEO text, and address can be changed easily later.
